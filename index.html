DIY Ideas for Cardboard

Creative Ways to Reuse Cardboard

Cardboard Boxes

Transform old cardboard boxes into storage organizers, playhouses for kids, or even custom gift boxes. Cut and paint them to add a personal touch to your home or office.

Cardboard Tubes

Use cardboard tubes from paper rolls to make pen holders, cable organizers,or DIY crafts like holiday decorations or wall art. They are lightweight yet sturdy for many creative uses.

Cardboard Sheets

Flattened cardboard sheets can be used to create photo frames, decorative wall panels, or even lightweight furniture. Layer and glue them for extra strength when needed.
